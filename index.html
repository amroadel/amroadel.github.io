Hello, I’m Amro Abdalla! I’m a second-year CS PhD student at Georgetown University. I am in the GUCV Research Group, advised by Sarah Adel Bargal. I am interested in Computer Vision and Generative AI, with a current focus on interpretability and safety of AI systems.

Previously, I worked as a data scientist at Dell Technologies in the 5G R&D team. I received my B.S.c. in Computer Engineering from the American University in Cairo in May 2022, where I was supervised by Mohamed Moustafa and Hesham Eraqi. I also graduated with minors in Mathematics and Business Administration.
